Donation

Donations of $2.00 or more to The Animal Rehoming Service Inc. (TARS) are Tax Deductible.

Every donation is gratefully received and is used as efficiently as possible. It helps continue the life saving work we do with unwanted dogs, cats and other animals. If you wish to donate, you can do so with Credit Card, Cheque/ Money Order or Direct Deposit.

When you decide which method you want to donate, just print out the Donation Form, fill out the section you choose and send this form along with your cheque/money order, credit card details or notification of your direct deposit to the address below, so that we can issue you with a receipt

The Animal Rehoming Service Inc.
P.O. Box 9028
Brington VIC 3186

For further information, please contact Michaela on 0409 21 31 31 or info@tars.org.au

Ritchies Community Benefits Program

The Animal Rehoming Service Inc. (TARS) is also part of the Ritchies Community Benefits program. You can nominate TARS as your preferred charity, so that whenever you shop at Ritchies, they will donate 1% of your shopping total to the service. This helps cover our veterinary, fostering and rehoming expenses.

Every donation is gratefully received and is used as efficiently as possible. It helps continue the life saving work we do with unwanted dogs, cats and other animals.

For further information, please contact Michaela on 0409 21 31 31 or info@tars.org.au
